Loans/Withdrawals - Overview

Key Concepts

When Loans are discussed it is also important to discuss Withdrawals that can be made with Annuities and other Investments. This is due to the fact that the Formulae are identical except for the small difference of a (\( +/- \)). Below are listed the Formulae

Constant Withdrawal : Interest Paid in Arrears

\( AV = C(1 + i)^n - X[\frac{(1 + i)^{n} -1 }{i}] \)

Constant Withdrawal : Interest Paid in Advance

\( AV = C(1 + i)^n - X(1 + i)[\frac{(1 + i)^{n} -1 }{i}] \)

Increasing Withdrawal : Interest Paid in Arrears

\( AV = C(1 + i)^n - X[\frac{(1 + i)^{n} - (1 + j)^{n}}{i - j}] \)

Increasing Withdrawal : Interest Paid in Advance

\( AV = C(1 + i)^n + X(1 + i)[\frac{(1 + i)^{n} - (1 + j)^{n}}{i - j}] \)

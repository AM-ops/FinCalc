Calculate the Annual Effective Rate of a Single Investment with Compound Interest

Formula is given by:

\( i = (\frac{AV}{PV})^{\frac{1}{n}} - 1 \)

Where:

  • \( i \) is the Annual Effective Interest Rate,
  • \( n \) is the total number of years,
  • \( AV \) is the Accumulated Value and
  • \( PV \) is the Present Value

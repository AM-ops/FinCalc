Calculate the Accumulated Value of a Increasing Annuity with Interest Paid in Advance

Formula is given by:

\( AV = C(1 + i)^n + X(1 + i)[\frac{(1 + i)^{n} - (1 + j)^{n}}{i - j}] \)

Where:

  • \( AV \) is the Accumulated Value,
  • \( C \) is the Initial Amount,
  • \( X \) is the Annuity Installment made every year,
  • \( i \) is the Annual Effective Interest Rate,
  • \( j \) is the Annual Increase in Installment and
  • \( n \) is the total number of years

Calculate the Nominal Per Period to Annual Effective Rate

\( i = (1 + \frac{i^{(p)}}{p})^p - 1 \)

  • \( i^{(p)} \) is the Nominal Per Period Rate and
  • \( p \) is the Compounding Period

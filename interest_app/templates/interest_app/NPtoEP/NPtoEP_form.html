Calculate the Nominal Per Period to Effective Per Period Rate

Formula is given by:

\( \frac{i^{(p_2)}}{p_2} = (1 + \frac{i^{(p_1)}}{p_1})^{\frac{p_1}{p_2}} - 1 \)

Where:

  • \( \frac{i^{(p_2)}}{p_2} \) is the Unknown Effective Per Period Rate,
  • \( i^{(p_1)} \) is the Known Nominal Per Period Rate,
  • \( p_1 \) is the Compounding Period for Known Rate and
  • \( p_2 \) is the Compounding Period for Unknown Rate

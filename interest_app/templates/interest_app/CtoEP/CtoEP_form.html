Calculate the Continuous to Effective Per Period Rate

Formula is given by:

\( \frac{i^{(p)}}{p} = e^{\frac{\delta}{p}} - 1 \)

Where:

  • \( \frac{i^{(p)}}{p} \) is the Effective Per Period Rate,
  • \( \delta \) is the Continuous Rate,
  • \( p \) is the Compounding Period and
  • \( e \) is Euler's number i.e. \( 2.7182818284... \)
